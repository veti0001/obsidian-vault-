- Risk and Uncertainty
	- Risk: events that are identifiable and quantifiable
	- Uncertainty (Knightian): unforeseeable events, impossible to quantify
	- Risk can be positive or negative
	- Goal of a company is to be risk neutral
- Apply top down
- Risk Identification:
	- depending on the risk can be established by senior employee or front end ones
- Risk analysis and Evaluation
	- key identified in the second steps are measured (model or any other methods) 
	- PESTLE:
		- political, economic ,social ,technical , legal, and environmental
		- identify external forces that impact organization (+ or -)
	- SWOT:
		- strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats
		- use the external forces identify in the PESTLE 
- Risk treatment
	- We can transfer, retain, avoid, reduce
