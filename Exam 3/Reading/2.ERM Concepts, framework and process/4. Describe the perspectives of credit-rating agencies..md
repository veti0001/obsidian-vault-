- AM methodology
	- main method is dialog between the company management and credit rating agency
	- conversation done by rating analyst
	- analyst looks at : 
		- strategic goals 
		- financial objective
		- management practices
	- Steps of analysis
		- Compile information
			- gather financial info, to set up specific meeting
			- gather more specific info in those meetings
			- use financial report and other public info
		- Perform analysis
			- takes into account all the relevant risks
			- compare to peers
		- Determine the rating:
			- rating committee deliberate on the rating given by the analyst
	- Rating published on there site
	- The rating are then monitored
	- ICR: 
		- entity capacity to meets its future financial obligation
		- credit risk rating
	- FSR:
		- insurer financial strength assessment
		- rate the ability to meet insurance policies and contract obligation.
	- IR;
		- degree of subordination of debt
		- when paid
