- **A.** Risk appetite:
	-  target level of risk in the corridor in normal circumstance
	-  risk reward trade-off targeted by the organization
	-  amount of risk that the company expects to take in order to meet its target
	-  set by the board of director
	-  mix of qualitative and quantitative statement
	- take into account internal and external factor 
	- Change over time
	- takes into account the risk profile of the company
- **G.** Risk Capacity:
	- Maximum amount of risk that a firm can support
- **H.** Risk tolerance:
	- upper and lower limit of risk corridor that the company is able to bolster
	- take into account internal and external factor
	-  set by the board of director
- **I.** Risk Profile
	- Snapshot of the actual risk exposure of the company
